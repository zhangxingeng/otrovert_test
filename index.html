What is the Otrovert Test?

The Otrovert Test is a 40‑question self‑assessment from The Gift of Not Belonging by Rami Kaminski. It helps you gauge your level of otroversion using a seven‑point scale from Very Strongly Agree to Very Strongly Disagree. Your answers are scored instantly on this page.

Results range from 40 to 280. As described in the book, a total score of 188 or higher suggests you are likely an otrovert.

Otrovert Test FAQ

How is it scored?

Each question is rated 1–7. Items are summed after accounting for direction. Scores ≥ 188 suggest you are likely an otrovert.
